StuAtt
Overview

An app that provides lecturers with an easier way to take attendance for physical lectures.

Description

This app enables lecturers take perfect attendance of students and eliminate false data entry by students who fill in for the absentees. It also ensures the lecturer is present for the lecture.

How it works.

  • The student installs the app,signs up and verifies their students' email,then they shall be able to access the QR code scanner.
  • The lecturer comes into the class with a QR code printed on paper(s).
  • With one click of the app and scan of the QR code, the student can be able to register their attendance which is recorded to an online database, thereby improving the data integrity of the attendance.
